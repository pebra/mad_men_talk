Some things about Mad Men

  • American drama television series by Matthew Weiner
  • premiered on July 19, 2007 on AMC
  • 7 seasons summing up to 92 episodes
  • final episode aired on May 17, 2015

The Setting

  • set primarly in the 1960's and 1970's
  • first at the fictional advertising agency Sterling Cooper on Madison Avenue, New York
  • Mad Men stands for the men working at advertising agencies on Madison Avenue
  • later at Sterling Cooper Draper Pryce on Sixth Avenue

The Story

The Story - Main Character

  • Don Draper is the Creative Director at Sterling Cooper
  • he is brilliant, but no one knows much about him
  • Don is married to Betty, but has several affairs during the seasons
  • excessive drinking and smoking is common

The Story - What happens?

  • get bigger clients, excel the competition
  • come up with innovative ideas
  • keep clients
  • fend hostile takeovers

The Story - How is it told?

  • story is told in a centripedal fashion
  • few new main characters are introduced
  • you learn a lot about the main characters during the seasons

Betty Draper

Betty

  • desires the picture perfect family life
  • centered around her husband and the kids
  • no career ambitions
  • following the traditional gender role model(propagated by the gov in the 50/60's)
  • no real problems
  • thinks her beauty is what keeps Don by her side
  • childish behaviour

Joan Holloway

  • Office Manager and Head of Secretaries at Sterling Cooper
  • has an affair with Roger Sterling
  • very feminine
  • self confident

Peggy Olson

  • came from Miss Deaver's secretarial school directly to Sterling Cooper
  • quite the opposite to Joan
  • doesn't want to be recognized for her femaleness, rather for her professional work
  • appears kind of cold to her coworkers
